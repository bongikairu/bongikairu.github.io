Yearbeast Tracker

PHP, HTML, and JS Hobby Project

New Bloom is a yearly event in Dota 2 . In 2015, this event allow players to join a special game mode during the year beast active time (20 minutes every 2-3 hours). Yearbeast Tracker is built to solve many problems, namely

  1. Allow estimate time of next year beast (became obsolete half-way through event)
  2. Allow player to track the beast without opening Dota 2
  3. Allow external services to benefit from tracking data (such as message alert to phone)

Through its lifespan of 3 weeks, it serves over 600,000 users with over 20 millions pageviews (According to Google Analytics). Cost of this operation, surprisingly, is under $50.
